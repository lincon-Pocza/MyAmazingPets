Remy

About

Remy is the son of my older sisters cat. As a baby he was very fluffy and small. This was a good enough reason for my other sister to fall in love and convince my mum to adopt him. Apon entering my home, he immediately gravitated towards Quazzi who became like his best friend/parent. Although he still preferred annoying me whenever he wanted to play.

LIKES

Remy’s favourite activity as a kitten was to run into my room in the middle of the night when everyone was asleep and jump from my desk, onto the blinds. No matter how many times he got stuck, he kept coming back to get stuck again, leaving me to rescue him. Thankfully, Remy now plays with Chase and Quazzi instead of waking me up.

FACTS

Remy was named by my sister for no particular reason. She just thought he looked like a Remy, whatever that means.


Favorite Activities

Remy has many activities that he enjoys. Here are a few examples of his favorite things to do in no particular order:

  • Playing with Quazzi
  • Running into my room to wake me up at night
  • Sleep
  • Chasing things outside
