Quazzi

ABOUT

We got Quazzi during the first COVID-19 lockdown in 2019 which is also what inspired his name. Before the lockdown, my dog Chase was getting lonely at home after the death of our other dog, Jet. Chase would have us coming home to find the contents of the kitchen bin spread out all over the floor (my Mum was never a fan of his artwork). During the lockdown my mum thought it would be the perfect time to get and train a cat for my dog to play with. As you can guess, they hated each other and would constantly fight over attention (the cat always won). Luckily they are now best friends and play together all day.

LIKES

Quazzi really loves sleeping. Unless someone else is trying to sleep of course as he thinks that is the best time to play and get attention. Quazzi like to roam outside doing whatever it is that cats do but he is always home for dinner as just like Garfield, he loves food.

FACTS

Quazzi was named by my mum after the COVID-19 lockdown as quazzi is short for quarantine.


Favorite Activities

Quazzi has many activities that he enjoys. Here are a few examples of his favorite things to do in no particular order:

  • Sleeping in places where he will not be found
  • playing with Remy and Chase
  • Keeping me awake by asking for constant attention
  • Eating food
